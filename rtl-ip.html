What you get

An inference engine built to integrate

The accelerator IP is designed for clean drop-in to a modern SoC, with the same standard interfaces you already use.

In-memory inference core

Computes inside the SRAM array to cut the weight-movement energy that dominates conventional NPUs on always-on workloads.

Standard SoC interfaces

AMBA AXI4 + AXI4-Lite with an IEEE 1685 IP-XACT register map as the single source of truth for your drivers and documentation.

Configurable & efficient

Configurable size and INT8–INT1 precision so you can tune the accelerator to your power, area and performance budget.
